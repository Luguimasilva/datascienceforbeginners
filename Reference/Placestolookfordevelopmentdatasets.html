Humanitarian data

  • UNOCHA’s HDX datastore
  • Crisislex archive of disaster tweets:
  • Citizen Action Team database
  • FAO Global Fire Information Management System. near-real-time data about fire.
  • UNOCHA ReliefWeb repository of crisis maps and information
  • UNHCR Statistical Online Population Database: refugees, asylum seekers, idps

Social media and network data

  • Hotchirp daily archive of tweets, by topic
  • Library of congress social media archive: promised, but never seen
  • Stanford large network datasets

Weather and climate data

  • Snow data
  • FAO Aquastat: repository of data about water

Conflict data

  • UN Peacekeeping statistics: fatalities etc

Development data

  • Quandl data repository, focussed on financial, economic and social indicators.
  • MeasureDHS demographic and health survey data results
  • UNDP human development reports 
  • UNICEF survey results, aka MICS
  • World Bank repository : development indicators etc.
